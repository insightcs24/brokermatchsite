1. Overview

Broker Match is an offline-first real estate CRM app for brokers/agents. The app is designed to store and process data locally on the user’s device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers.

Broker Match stores user-entered data locally on your device, including:

  • Buyer details (name, phone, preferences, notes, lead status)
  • Property and seller details (name, phone, listing data, notes)
  • Reminder information
  • App settings (theme, country preference, PIN lock preference, templates)
  • Locally selected images for property listings

This data remains on your device unless you explicitly export/share it.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used only for app functionality, such as:

  • Managing buyers and properties
  • Matching buyers with suitable properties
  • Scheduling local reminder notifications
  • Generating local PDF/backup exports
  • Enabling call/WhatsApp/share actions initiated by the user

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party servers.

Data may be shared only when you explicitly trigger user actions such as:

  • WhatsApp/share
  • Phone dialer launch
  • Exporting backup files (JSON/CSV/PDF)

5. Permissions Used

Broker Match may request the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: to show reminder alerts
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: to trigger reminders at exact times (where supported)
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: to select and attach local property images
  • (Legacy) WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (older Android): for compatibility with file flows

Permissions are used solely to provide app features requested by the user.

6. Data Security

Because Broker Match is offline-first, data is stored locally on device storage. We do not transmit app data to our servers.

Users are responsible for device-level security (screen lock, backups, etc.). Optional in-app PIN lock is available.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained locally until the user edits, deletes, clears app data, or uninstalls the app.

Users can delete records in-app and control backups manually.

8. Children’s Privacy

Broker Match is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Third-Party Services

Broker Match uses third-party libraries for local app functionality (for example, local notifications, file handling, sharing, URL launching). These libraries are used within the app workflow and do not imply backend data collection by us.

10. International Users

If you use Broker Match outside[Country], your data still remains on your device unless you choose to share/export it.
